Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use a Bubble Blast?

Tap the blue bubble button in the bottom-right corner to trap every enemy on screen. You start each run with 3 — grab a submarine to refill them!

How do I unlock new skins?

Earn coins by eating fish and diving deep, then tap a skin in the menu to unlock it. Some special skins are available as a purchase.

I bought a skin but don't see it.

Open the menu and tap Restore in the top-left to restore your purchases.

Is the game safe for kids?

Yes! There are no ads and no data collection, and all purchases are protected behind a parental gate. See our Privacy Policy.
